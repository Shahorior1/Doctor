About Dr. Abdullah Al Mosabbir

Leading Hematologist & Researcher

Dr. Abdullah Al Mosabbir is a distinguished hematologist with over 15 years of experience in treating complex blood disorders. He combines clinical expertise with groundbreaking research to provide the most advanced care for his patients.

Education & Training

  • MD, Hematology - Johns Hopkins University
  • Fellowship in Bone Marrow Transplantation - MD Anderson Cancer Center
  • Residency in Internal Medicine - Mayo Clinic
  • Bachelor of Medicine - Dhaka Medical College

Current Affiliations

  • Chief of Hematology - City General Hospital
  • Research Director - Blood Disorders Institute
  • Professor of Medicine - University Medical School
  • Board Member - International Hematology Society

Awards & Recognition
